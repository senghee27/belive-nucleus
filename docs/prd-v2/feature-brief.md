# BeLive Nucleus — What It Does (Team Brief)

**For:** Operations team, cluster leads, IOEs, OOEs, Technicians
**Date:** 7 April 2026
**Written by:** Lee Seng Hee

---

## What Is Nucleus?

Nucleus is a new system that helps Lee manage all 11 clusters more effectively. It reads every message in your Lark cluster groups, understands what's happening, and helps Lee respond faster.

**You don't need to do anything differently.** Keep using Lark exactly the same way. Nucleus works in the background.

---

## What Changed

### Lee sees everything faster

Every message you send in your cluster group is captured by Nucleus. Lee can now see the full picture across all 11 clusters in one screen — who has issues, how old they are, who's handling them.

### Lee responds faster

When you report a problem in the group, Nucleus helps Lee draft a response. He reviews it and sends it himself. This means you'll get Lee's reply faster than before — sometimes within minutes of reporting.

### Morning briefings are automatic

Every morning at 8:00am, your cluster gets a Pre-Standup Brief — a card in the group showing today's open tickets, overnight highlights, and urgent items. This comes from Lee's account.

In the evening, an OCC Nightly Review card arrives with today's wins and areas to improve.

### Lee knows your cluster health

There's a health score for every cluster (0-100). It tracks maintenance tickets, cleaning, move-ins, and turnarounds. If your cluster has overdue tickets or silent periods, the score drops and Lee can see it immediately.

---

## What This Means Day to Day

1. **Keep posting your standup reports** in the cluster group as usual. Nucleus detects them and tracks compliance.

2. **Keep reporting issues** in the group. Lee will see them faster and respond sooner.

3. **When Lee sends a message**, it really is Lee. He reviewed it before sending. It's not a bot.

4. **If you get tagged (@) in a message from Lee**, it means Nucleus detected that you're relevant to the issue and Lee confirmed the tag.

5. **The morning brief card** in your group is generated by AI but reviewed by Lee before it goes out. Read it — it summarizes what's open and what needs attention today.

---

## What Happens If Something Goes Wrong

- If Nucleus misclassifies an issue, Lee will catch it during review. No wrong messages get sent without Lee seeing them first.

- If the morning brief doesn't arrive, Lee will know immediately (the system tracks delivery).

- If you notice anything unusual in the group messages (wrong info, strange tags), tell Lee directly. The system is still learning.

---

## Nothing Changes for You

You don't need to:
- Install anything
- Learn a new system
- Change how you report
- Use any new tools

Just keep doing your job the same way. Nucleus helps Lee help you faster.
